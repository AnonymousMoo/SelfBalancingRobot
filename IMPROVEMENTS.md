What I've learned from this project and what can be done better:
1) Pay more attention to mechanical design, some alterations in this could be to make it taller and to keep in mind electronics while designing.
   For instance, the boards I used could've fit much better if the base was a bit wider.
2) Obviously, trying out LQR rather than PID would be nice but at this stage, I am not familiar enough with LQR to proceed.
3) Actually making the RC component of this robot.
   Due to extenuating circumstances, currently I have no time for this, but will come back later and make an app and implement remote controlled turning and moving.
5) While implementing bluetooth control, currently the target angle is just set a bit to the left or right to simulate backward and forward motion.
   This could be better achieved by cascading PID, but again, at this stage, I am not familiar enough with it to proceed.
6) It would be much more convenient to make a PCB for this rather than wiring as that led to many problems.
   Another thing to improve if I come back to this project.
7) Make a BOM for the electrical components and put it here.
